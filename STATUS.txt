───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
WHAT WAS FIXED
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

File: .htaccess
Problem Lines Removed:
  - <DirectoryMatch "config">
  - <DirectoryMatch "vendor">
  - <Directory ~ "(config|vendor)">

These directives are only allowed in Apache's main config,
not in .htaccess files.

Replaced with FilesMatch directive (allowed in .htaccess)
